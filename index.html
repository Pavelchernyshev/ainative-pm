01 — Why now

Using AI is no longer a specialty.
It’s the baseline.

Shopify put it in a company-wide memo: “using AI is now a baseline expectation.” Meta added a “Product Sense with AI” stage to its PM interviews. OpenAI runs all of its products with roughly 25 high-agency PMs. The job didn’t disappear — it compressed, and the leverage moved to the people who build.

The market followed. Lenny’s State of the Product Job Market (Mar 2026) found PM openings at a three-year high while demand for AI PM roles is, in his words, “hockey-sticking.” The gap isn’t headcount. It’s skills: writing evals, shipping prototypes instead of documents, and orchestrating agents.

This course closes that gap — not with theory, but with the exact working habits named again and again by the people running product at OpenAI, Anthropic, and the best AI-native teams.
