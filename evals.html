02 — What you'll ship

Three milestones. Three reviews.
One suite your team can trust.

1

Golden dataset + graders

Days 1–4. Thirty real cases from your product — typical, edge, and failure — each with an expected outcome and a grader assigned: code check, human, or LLM-as-judge. Reviewed within 48h.

2

Calibrated judge + baseline

End of week 1. Your LLM-judge rubric, calibrated against your own ratings until you trust it — then the first full baseline run: a pass rate your product has never had before. Reviewed.

3

Regression gates + eval report

End of week 2. Launch thresholds your team agrees to, a before/after report on one real change, and the eval habit wired into your launch checklist. Reviewed — and yours forever.
